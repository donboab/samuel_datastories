Virality's Key Driver

Social Media Engagement Dashboard
Tracking Engagement Trends Across Platforms, Content Types, and Regions

Goal

In today’s fast-moving digital world, one viral post can change everything for a brand. I was brought into a project where the marketing team wanted answers to a simple but powerful question, " What actually makes a social media post go viral and how can we repeat that success?"

My task was to dig into a dataset of 5,000 viral posts across TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube, and uncover the patterns hidden in the numbers. Which platforms are driving the most engagement? What kind of content catches fire? And which regions are showing the most interaction? To bring these answers to life, I built an interactive Power BI dashboard designed for everyday use by marketers and content creators. It doesn't just show data but it tells a story. You can instantly see the top-performing platforms, the most engaging content types, and the countries where posts are taking off. I also added custom measures like total engagement, top engaged Post_ID, and visuals like average engagement by content type and regional engagement by top 5 countries. I created this clear, easy-to-understand tool that helps teams make smart, fast decisions about where to focus their time, energy, and creativity.

Tools & Skills Used:

  • Power BI: Built the entire dashboard using Power BI, including interactive charts, slicers, and charts.
  • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Created custom measures for top performing platform, most engaging content type, total engagement, top engaged post_ID etc.
  • Data cleaning and transformation
  • Power Query
  • Data visualization and dashboard design
  • Analytical thinking and Storytelling with Data

Project Description

Using Power BI, I built an interactive dashboard that turns raw data into meaningful insights. It’s designed to be easy to use, even for non-technical users, so teams can quickly spot patterns and make smarter decisions. Some of the main features include:

  • Top Performing Platform Shows which platform generated the most engagement overall, helping the team double down on what’s working.
  • Most Engaging Content Type Breaks down content formats (like Reels, Shorts, Tweets, Video etc.) to reveal which ones consistently drive strong interaction.
  • Regional Engagement by Country Highlights the top five countries with the highest engagement, giving insight into where content is resonating globally.
  • Average Engagement Rate by Content Type Helps compare how different content styles are performing in terms of views, likes, comments, and shares.
  • Engagement Level by Platform Displays how each platform stacks up. I got the value as posts labeled (Low, Medium, or High engagement), however, these values were converted to numbers in order to help me calculate the result in percentage
  • Top Engaged Post_ID Pinpoints individual posts that stood out and sparked the most attention.
To make exploration easier, the dashboard includes filters so users can drill into the data by platform, region, content type, and Hastags.

Key findings

  • Platform Performance: Identified the social media platform(s) generating the highest overall engagement. This insight gave the team clarity on where to prioritize content efforts and media spend. Twitter platform drove 14% more engagement with videos than other platforms.
  • Content Format Trends: Revealed which types of content (e.g., Reels, Shorts, Tweets) consistently outperformed others in likes, shares, and comments. This helped guide creative direction and content planning across campaigns.
  • Top Post Insights: Surfaced individual posts (Post_IDs) with the highest engagement, giving the team real examples of what success looks like and providing templates for future campaigns.
  • Regional Hotspots:
    • Highlighted the top 5 countries with the highest levels of user interaction, pointing to specific regions where content was resonating most. These insights supported more targeted regional strategies. It was discovered that the United States gives most of the engagements.
  • Engagement Quality: Classified posts by Low, Medium, and High engagement, showing how different platforms stack up in terms of content quality and user response. This allowed for benchmarking and refinement of platform-specific strategies.
  • Engagement by Hashtag & Region (Heatmap): A heatmap visualization revealed the most effective hashtag-region combinations, uncovering where certain trends were gaining traction and helping the team align hashtags and content with the right audiences.
